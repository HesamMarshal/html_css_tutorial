Contrasts

WCAG 2.0 level AA requires at least:

Normal Text: 4.5:1
Large Text: 3:1

WCAG 2.1:

Graphics and user interface components 3:1
(such as form input borders).

WCAG Level AAA

Requires a contrast ratio of at least
Normal text: 7:1
Large text: 4.5:1
Large text is defined as 14 point (typically 18.66px) and bold or larger, or 18 point (typically 24px) or larger.
